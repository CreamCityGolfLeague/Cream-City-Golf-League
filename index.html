Rules & Structure

League Overview
  • Season: April–September 2026
  • Events: 10 Individual Events + 1 Scramble + 1 Par 3 Special + 1 Championship
  • Rotating courses for variety and challenge
Event Types
  • Individual: Stroke play using net scores (GHIN-based handicaps)
  • Midseason Scramble: Four-person scramble; does not affect individual standings
  • Par 3 Challenge: Full month to play a designated Par 3; best net score counts
  • Championship: Double points
Handicaps & Flights
  • Maintain a GHIN Index; otherwise, app-based handicap after 3 rounds
  • Flights (by index): A (0.0–6.9), B (7.0–12.9), C (13.0–18.9), D (19.0+)
  • Monthly review for flight adjustments
Scoring & Points
  • Points awarded per placement within each flight (1,000 total per round)
  • Attendance earns minimum points; Championship awards double points
Payouts
  • Per flight: 1st Net, 2nd Net, Low Gross
  • Ties broken via USGA methods; digital gift card payouts
Rules of Play
  • USGA rules + local rules apply; all putts holed
  • Tees: A/B/C ~6,500 yds; D plays next set below; 55+ may use senior tees
  • Players cannot play with someone more than 3 times (unless in a foursome)
Administration
  • Apps: Unknown Golf or 18Birdies for scoring and handicaps
  • Transparent costs; Venmo for payments; reminders before each window
